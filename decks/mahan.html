Naval Officer and Lecturer

Alfred Mahan, born in 1840, served as a naval officer from 1859 to 1885 and later became a lecturer at Naval War College

Author of Books

Mahan published 4 books that made him world-renowned authority in everything naval

1890: The Influence of Sea Power upon History, 1660–1783

1892: The Influence of Sea Power upon the French Revolution and Empire, 1793–1812

1897: The Life of Nelson

1905: Sea Power in Relation to the War of 1812
